## Before Class - Complete Slide 1

**Slide title:** Ethical Framework Snapshot

Include the following:

1. Your name.
2. One central personal ethical principle (from your Personal Ethical Position).
3. Two professional ethical commitments (from your Professional Ethical Position).
4. One professional boundary: something you would not build, support, deploy, or approve without further ethical review.
5. One unresolved tension that affects ethical reasoning or professional practice.

### Important Framing for Slide 1

1. Keep this professional-facing and concise.
2. Slide 1 is a snapshot, not a summary of the full assignment.
3. Slide 1 is provisional and is expected to be stress-tested and possibly revised in class.
4. BRIEF is the core requirement: your content must fit cleanly on one slide and be easy to read at a glance.
5. Suggested ways to keep it brief: use short line fragments, avoid paragraphs, and keep each line short.
6. If content is too long, reduce wording rather than shrinking text.
7. State positions as decision rules rather than broad value words.
8. Example of stronger phrasing: "I will disclose meaningful AI use" is stronger than "transparency."
9. Do not overload the slide visually. This is a presentation artifact, not a document.
10. If you use terms like fairness, transparency, or accountability, briefly indicate what they mean in practice.
11. You do not need to disclose private or personal details.
