## Why This Content Was Separated

The game development activity was repositioned as an **optional, separate lab assignment** to:
1. Allow students to focus on **synthesis of A7/8/9 analysis** (Assignment 10 priority)
2. Enable students catching up on prior assignments to recover
3. Preserve all game material without losing it or adding workload
4. Create space for the game to be completed later or integrated into Week 11–14 enrichment activities

## Connection to Course

This temporal mediation work remains valuable and could be deployed as:
- A **Week 11 Lab** — students use BBS concepts to analyze the temporal structure of their own case studies
- An **optional enrichment** — students interested in game design and ethics extend their A7/8/9 analysis through game rules
- A **peer teaching activity** — students design rule changes based on what they learned about responsibility and capability
