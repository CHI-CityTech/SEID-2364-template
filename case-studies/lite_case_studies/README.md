# SEID 2364 LITE Case Studies

This folder contains the "lite" versions of case studies available for final projects and other assignments in SEID 2364. Each case provides a concise scenario and framing, leaving core ethical and governance analysis to the student.

## Case Study Summaries

**CS001-L: Moral Machine and Autonomous Vehicle Ethics**  
Explores ethical tradeoffs in autonomous vehicle decision-making, focusing on dilemmas where harm cannot be fully avoided. Considers public values, governance, and the complexity of real-world deployment.

**CS002-L: Emergency Room Triage During Resource Scarcity**  
Examines how hospitals allocate limited resources (like ICU beds) during crises. Focuses on survival probabilities, fairness, and the impact on patients, families, and institutional trust.

**CS003-L: Eminent Domain for Infrastructure Development**  
Analyzes the ethics of government acquisition of private property for public infrastructure. Considers displacement, compensation, and the balance between collective benefit and local harm.

**CS004-L: Grading Curves in Competitive Academic Programs**  
Investigates the use of grading curves in education, their impact on fairness, student stress, collaboration, and institutional goals.

**CS005-L: Mandatory Vaccination for Public School Attendance**  
Considers public health policy requiring vaccination for school attendance, weighing public safety, autonomy, trust, and policy legitimacy.

**CS006-L: AI Academic Writing Assistant**  
Looks at the use of AI tools for writing support in education, addressing issues of authorship, learning integrity, and institutional governance.

**CS007-L: AI Hiring Assistant**  
Focuses on algorithmic screening in employment, examining fairness, bias, accountability, and the impact on job applicants and hiring practices.

**CS008-L: AI Loan and Credit Advisor**  
Explores AI-driven credit risk assessment in financial services, considering access to finance, risk scoring, and institutional responsibility.

**CS009-L: AI Medical Triage Chatbot**  
Analyzes digital health tools for symptom triage, focusing on care access, safety, and the challenges of supporting diverse users.

**CS010-L: Loan Signal Tracking Scenario**  
Examines how information about loan applications moves through various actors and systems, highlighting issues of trust, transformation, and decision integrity.

**CS011-L: COVID-19 Misinformation Moderation**  
Studies platform interventions during the pandemic (labeling, removal, redirection), considering public health, platform governance, and communication ethics.

**CS012-L: OptiRate Workplace Analytics**  
Looks at AI-enabled workplace analytics for productivity and burnout, focusing on employee privacy, organizational governance, and ethical use of behavioral data.

**CS013-L: Quantum Musico and Meta-Mozart**  
Explores generative AI in music, cultural heritage, and creative governance, including issues of rights, authorship, and recursive training.

**CS014-L: Ethical Distance and Virtual Locality in Social Media**  
Analyzes how social-media content travels across platforms and contexts, raising questions about harm, visibility, and platform governance.
