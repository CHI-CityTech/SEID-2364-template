## Column Definitions

**Introduced**  
The first point in the course where the concept is explicitly presented to students.

**Expanded**  
Sessions or discussions where the concept is explored more deeply.

**Applied**  
Exercises, case studies, or experiments where students actively use the concept.

**Revisited**  
Later points in the course where the concept reappears in a new context.

**Related Assignments**  
Assignments or journal work where the concept is explicitly used.

**Technical / Analytical Concepts**  
Connections to computational, engineering, or analytical frameworks.

**Pedagogical Function**  
Why the concept appears at that point in the course and what intellectual shift it is intended to produce.
