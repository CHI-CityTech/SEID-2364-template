# SEID 2364 Exemplar Case Study
## Selected Exemplar: COVID-19 Misinformation Moderation

## Selection Status
This selection is now finalized.

## Exemplar Decision
The course exemplar case study is:
- COVID-19 Misinformation Moderation

## Why This Case Was Selected
This case is the strongest exemplar because it combines:
- clear socio-technical mediation pathways,
- high stakeholder complexity,
- strong bidirectional risk structure,
- direct alignment with professional codes (ACM and IEEE),
- explicit governance tradeoffs across institutional and jurisdictional contexts,
- existing use in Assignment 11 and Week 11 lesson flow.

It therefore models the full analytical depth expected in SEID 2364 and can serve as the benchmark for student case development.
